Are you very comfortable with decimals, fractions, and percents?

Do not take Module 0 first, because Module 0 is a wide-ranging introduction to many subject areas in beyond-curricular middle school math. So, Module 0 requires knowledge about many different topics, which you may not have seen. Instead, start with Module 1, whose prerequisite knowledge is narrower (only fractions, decimals, and percents). After finishing Module 1, even young students will gain an early start in developing strong mathematical maturity, and learning how to think and ask questions about unfamiliar concepts. You can then take Module 0 to see how interesting and broad math is, or you can take our Workout 1A and 1B courses which provide further practice and challenge on those algebraic topics. (You can also take Module 0 and Workouts 1A+1B simultaneously.)

You may be too young for our courses. Learn decimals, fractions, and percents first, and then you'll be able to start.
